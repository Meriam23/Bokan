ABOUT BOKAN

CHEF GUILLAUME

Guillaume Gillan, Executive Chef at Bōkan, brings diverse international experience to the acclaimed sky-high restaurant in Canary Wharf. Set on the 37th floor, the restaurant focuses on contemporary European dining using locally sourced, British ingredients. Growing up in the beautiful city of Nice in the South of France, Guillaume has been passionate about good food since childhood.
Growing up, he became friend with Florian, whom he met in Monaco. We are lucky to have him as our beloved Restaurant Manager. Together, they are passionated about giving you the best experience and it shows.

RESTAURANT - 37th

Indulge and dine amongst industrial dockyard sophistication whilst admiring captivating views of London’s skyline. Bōkan 37 creates a warm, inviting & relaxed dining experience with a menu that will take your taste-buds on a journey of European discovery. Florian and his team are proud to offer you an experience that you won't forget.

BOTTOMLESS BRUNCH - £52 PER PERSON

With a backdrop of stunning views, kick start your weekend with a lavish bottomless brunch. Chef Guillaume has created a seasonal and stylish three-course menu to enjoy alongside endless prosecco in the laid-back yet luxe Bōkan 37 Restaurant.

* Menus are subject to change as Chef Guillaume and his brigade offer you only the best seasonal produce to your table.

BAR - 38th

Sip unique cocktails, wines, spirits & craft beers in laid-back luxury on the 38th floor.

Anchored in the docklands, the award winning bar at Bōkan 38 champions locally distilled spirits, intriguing cocktails, carefully chosen craft beers and an extensive wine list. Admire the impressive views of Canary Wharf and the City of London, whilst indulging in the unique flavours of our enticing food & drink menus.
*Only card payments are accepted. Entry is subject to capacity in line with COVID Government regulations. Alcohol can only be served alongside with food.

ROOFTOP - 39th

An easy-going & informal hideaway, transporting you far from the buzz of the busy streets below. The bar and roof terrace at Bōkan 39 features a specialist gin bar, rooftop daybeds, perfect for intimate gatherings, whilst admiring striking views of London.

Experience impressive views of London at the highest rooftop bar on the 39th floor at Bōkan. Escape to a world far removed from the city below. A hidden gem waiting to be discovered. Lounge in the sky all year round. Perch inside at the rooftop bar or unwind on the terrace.
